Vision

Content:
The Special Education Department exists to provide leadership and service to equip campuses with the knowledge and skills in order for students to create their own future success. The department believes in the power and importance of collaboration with campus staff, parents and students to provide a world class education for all our special education students in Katy ISD. To be recognized as the Special Education department that provides a level playing field, for all the special education students in order to have access to the general education curriculum from a continuum of service delivery models in Katy ISD.
