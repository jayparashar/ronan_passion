Katy ISD Health Services supports the education of all students by collaborating with families and medical providers to meet the needs of students and staff while promoting optimum health and wellness in the learning community. Katy ISD Health Services is committed to improving health outcomes and academic achievement for all students. Through early identification of medical issues and providing coordinated health care, an optimum level of health can be achieved for all students.
