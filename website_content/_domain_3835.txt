The Katy community continues to be recognized as one of the fastest growing residential communities in the nation. Families continue to choose Katy ISD as a quality school district for their children, in a large part due to the excellent quality of facilities and learning environments. The Facilities, Planning, and Construction Department is comprised of 13 highly dedicated and skilled staff members, including four office support personnel, who each embody the vision and mission for the department; the focus of which is the creation of outstanding teaching and learning environments for our students and staff.
